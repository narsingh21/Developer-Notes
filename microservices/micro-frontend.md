## why Micro Frontend?
- Welcome to you if you are reading this file. why we requried micro frontend.  We often work with single monolithic apps. One repo and one code-base. But challenges comes code base grows hundreds of components. many component won't required by many modules or features.
Multiple teams working on single code with diffrent modules .They have diffrent deploment.

Now the Microfrontend comes like thor comes in Avengers. To solve all those problems.

It seprate modules or features and their dependecies. you can work with sepratly in isolation without other part of code. you can assume like in mission imposible tom curse team members work seprate problems independently and when all united the picture will be end with the solution.
